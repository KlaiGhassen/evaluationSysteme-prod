1 - Clarity of instruction:

Does the teacher clearly explain concepts and provide examples to help students understand?

2 - Engagement:

Does the teacher actively engage students in the learning process, encouraging participation and asking questions?

3 - Availability and accessibility:

Is the teacher available outside of class to provide extra help or answer questions, and are they approachable and willing to listen to student concerns?

4 - Support for diverse learning styles:

Does the teacher use a variety of teaching methods and accommodate different learning styles in the classroom?

5 - Fairness:

Does the teacher treat all students equally and fairly, and do they hold high expectations for all students?

6 - Professionalism:

Does the teacher show up on time and prepared for class, and do they maintain a positive and respectful classroom environment?
